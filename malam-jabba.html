Overview

Pakistan's Premier Mountain Resort

Malam Jabba is Pakistan's only international-standard ski resort, perched at 2,804 metres above sea level in the heart of Swat Valley. What was once a hideaway for royalty is now one of the country's most thrilling year-round destinations.

In winter, the slopes transform into a glistening white paradise attracting skiers and snowboarders from across Pakistan. In summer, the same mountains offer breathtaking hiking trails, lush green meadows, and some of the cleanest mountain air you'll ever breathe.

The resort features a modern chairlift, ski school, equipment rentals, and a comfortable hotel — making it accessible to both beginners and seasoned adventurers.

✨ Why Visit Malam Jabba?

  • Pakistan's only operating ski chairlift
  • Stunning 360° panoramic mountain views
  • Summer hiking trails through pine forests
  • Paragliding, zipline & adventure sports
  • On-site hotel, restaurant & ski school
  • Perfect for families, couples & groups

Getting There

How To Reach Malam Jabba

🚗

By Car

From Mingora via Saidu Sharif Road → Khwazakhela → Malam Jabba. About 1.5–2 hrs on a scenic mountain road.

🚌

By Hiace / Van

Shared Hiaces depart daily from Mingora's main bus stand. Ask for "Malam Jabba" — journey takes ~2 hrs.

🚁

By Helicopter

Charter helicopter tours from Saidu Sharif Airport available for groups — the most spectacular way to arrive!

📍

GPS Coordinates

34.8167° N, 72.5667° E — Search "Malam Jabba Ski Resort" on Google Maps for turn-by-turn directions.

Visitor Tips

Before You Go

🧥

Dress WarmEven in summer, temperatures can drop sharply at 2,800m. Always bring layers and a windproof jacket.

💵

Carry CashATMs are not available at the resort. Withdraw cash in Mingora before heading up the mountain.

🕗

Start EarlyLeave Mingora by 7–8 AM to avoid traffic on mountain roads and get the most out of your day.

📶

Limited SignalMobile coverage is weak at the resort. Download offline maps before you leave town.

🍱

Pack SnacksResort food options are limited. Pack snacks and water, though the on-site dhaba serves basic meals.

📅

Book AheadHotel rooms at the resort sell out fast in ski season (Dec–Feb). Reserve at least 2 weeks in advance.
