Overview

Swat's Beloved Green Escape

Fizagat Park is one of Swat Valley's most cherished public spaces — a beautiful riverside park stretching along the lush banks of the Swat River, just 8 km from the heart of Mingora. It is the go-to destination for families, couples and picnickers from across the region.

The park is famous for its manicured lawns, towering shade trees, colourful flowerbeds and the soothing sound of the Swat River flowing alongside. Walking paths wind through the greenery, and children's play areas make it a paradise for young visitors.

Whether you're looking for a peaceful morning stroll, a riverside picnic, or simply a place to relax and breathe fresh mountain air — Fizagat Park delivers an experience that feels like a world away from the busy bazaars of Mingora.

Park Zones

What's Inside the Park

Fizagat Park has something for everyone — explore its different areas.

🌸

Flower Gardens

Seasonal flower beds with roses, marigolds and local wildflowers. Especially stunning in April and May when everything blooms.

🌊

Riverside Promenade

A paved walking path running along the Swat Riverbank — perfect for morning walks with mountain views across the water.

🎡

Children's Play Area

Swings, slides and climbing frames for young children. A safe, open space where kids can play freely while parents relax nearby.

🧺

Picnic Lawns

Vast open lawns under the shade of mature trees — the ideal spot to spread a blanket and enjoy a family meal outdoors.

🌳

Forest Walk

A shaded trail winding through a grove of old trees. Cool, peaceful and perfect for an afternoon escape from the summer heat.

☕

Refreshment Stalls

Local vendors sell snacks, cold drinks, ice cream and Kehwa tea throughout the park. Fresh corn on the cob is a local favourite!

Getting There

How To Reach Fizagat Park

🚗

By Car

From Mingora, head north along the Swat River road towards Bahrain. Fizagat Park is clearly signposted on the left after 8 km.

🛺

By Rickshaw

Auto-rickshaws from Mingora's Green Chowk drop you right at the park gate. Expect to pay Rs 100–150 one way.

🚌

By Wagon

Any Hiace wagon heading towards Bahrain or Kalam will pass Fizagat. Ask the driver to stop at "Fizagat" — fare Rs 30–50.

📍

GPS Coordinates

34.7920° N, 72.3850° E — Search "Fizagat Park Swat" on Google Maps for turn-by-turn directions.

Visitor Tips

Before You Visit

🌅

Visit Early or at SunsetMornings are peaceful and cool. Sunsets over the river are magical. Avoid midday in summer when it gets crowded and hot.

🧺

Bring Your Own FoodWhile vendors are available, a homemade picnic in the park is far more enjoyable. Pack local snacks and a thermos of Kehwa.

🏖

Weekends Are BusyThe park fills up on Fridays and Saturdays. Visit Sunday to Thursday for a quieter, more relaxed experience.

🚗

Free ParkingThere is a free car park adjacent to the main entrance. Arrive before 10 AM to guarantee a spot on busy days.

🌿

Keep it CleanFizagat is a public treasure. Use the bins provided and take all rubbish with you when you leave — lead by example.

🌸

Spring is BestVisit in April–May when the flower beds are in full bloom and the river is running full with snowmelt — simply breathtaking.
